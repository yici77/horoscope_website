The Story Behind

About This Website

A data-driven horoscope aggregation website — where ancient cosmic wisdom meets modern statistical analysis.

☽ ✦ ☾

Why This Exists

✦ ☽ ✦

Horoscope content is one of the most widely consumed forms of entertainment online — yet different websites often offer conflicting predictions for the same zodiac sign in the same week. This raised a question: is there a way to look across multiple sources, find common patterns, and present them as data? This project explores that idea — collecting horoscope articles from various websites, analyzing them with AI, and visualizing the results to reveal where sources agree, where they diverge, and what the numbers actually say.

How It Works

01

Web Scraping

Each week, horoscope articles are silently gathered from multiple astrology websites across the web — aggregating diverse voices into a single constellation of data.

02

AI Analysis

Each article is interpreted by an AI language model, which classifies horoscope ratings across five dimensions — Love, Career, Wealth, Health, and Overall — revealing the hidden signals within the prose.

03

Statistical Analysis

Ratings from multiple horoscope sources are aggregated and statistically interpreted across five dimensions — Love, Career, Wealth, Health, and Overall — then visualized through charts to highlight weekly positive fortune trends across the zodiac signs.

04

Visualization & Delivery

The processed data is rendered into charts and delivered through an interactive website — giving each zodiac sign its own weekly energy portrait, grounded in data rather than divination.
